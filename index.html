About I-DLE

i-dle (formerly known as (G)I-DLE) is a South Korean girl group formed by CUBE Entertainment. They debuted in 2018 and quickly gained attention for their authentic and self-produced style, with leader Soyeon leading much of the group's songwriting and production. In May 2025, after seven years of activity, the group officially dropped the "G" from their name, rebranding as simply i-dle. This change marks a new, freer and more mature era, moving away from the gendered label implied by the "G", which originally stood for "girl". Despite the new name, i-dle maintains the same lineup: Miyeon, Minnie, Soyeon, Yuqi and Shuhua, continuing to establish themselves as one of the most influential girl groups of K-pop’s 4th generation.
